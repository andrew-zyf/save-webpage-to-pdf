Opening Section

Saved articles need to feel calm on paper. This paragraph is intentionally long enough to show two-sided alignment, hyphenation, line rhythm, and the way links sit inside normal prose without shouting at the reader.

The goal is not decoration. The page should have a quiet archive quality: readable text, restrained metadata, and enough contrast that quotes, captions, and tables can be scanned without breaking the flow.

A blockquote should feel like a considered aside, not a loud interruption. The paper tint and accent rule give it shape while keeping the tone restrained.
